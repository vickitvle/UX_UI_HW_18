ABOUT ME /

Who is she?
Why hello! My name's Vicki Le and I'm a UX designer with a background in sociology and making a mean iced latte (or a nice hot one).

Why UX?
After graduating from UF with a B.A. in Sociology and spending 3 subsequent years as a barista, I came to the realization that I love connecting with people and learning about what brings them joy, what frustrates them, and how I can help them. Intertwining my interests in visual design and connecting with others led me to UX/UI design, where I hope to create meaningful experiences with people for people.

What does she do? When she is not learning about UX/UI Design, she is watching vlogs on YouTube, watching anime (currently: One Piece), reading (favorite: Pachinko), journaling, or creating Spotify playlists.
