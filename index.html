ABOUT ME /

Vicki (Thuy-Vi) Le is a UX designer with a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology (UF c/o 2017). Her education, work as a barista, and love for engaging visual design has driven her passion towards connecting with people, and learning about their motivations, thought-processes, and frustrations, eventually leading to her current enrollment in the UX/UI Design Bootcamp at Columbia.

Although her life goal is to own a quaint coffee shop that brings together people from all walks of life, she is currently pursuing a career in UX Design and is looking to work alongside creative teams to build meaningful experiences and sustainable solutions for people (from all walks of life).

When she is not learning about UX/UI Design, she is watching vlogs on YouTube, watching anime (currently: One Piece), reading (favorite: Pachinko), journaling, or creating Spotify playlists.
