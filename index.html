ABOUT ME /

Who am I?
My name's Vicki Le, and I'm a UX designer with a background in sociology and making a mean iced latte (or a nice hot one).

Q: What does that have to do with UX?
After graduating from UF with a B.A. in Sociology and spending 3 subsequent years as a barista, I've discovered that connecting with people and learning about what brings them joy, what frustrates them, and how I can help them basically defines my existence. Intertwining my interests in visual design and connecting with others led me to UX/UI design, where I hope to create meaningful experiences with people for people.

Q: What do you do?
From user research to high-fidelity prototypes and iterative designs, I'm continuously learning about the world of UX/UI and the multitude of ways it can alleviate the challenges of everyday life not just for myself, but for everyone.

Q: What do you look for?
I thrive in collaborative and creatively driven environments where I can connect with people who share mutual interests and respect one another. Continuing to grow, learn, and using the skills I've learned to positively impact those around me and beyond the screen would be the ultimate achievement.
